Some random information

Luffy is the main character of One Piece, he is the captain of the strawhat pirates and his dream is to become the king of the pirates by finding the one piece, the greatest treasure in the world.

Zoro is the first mate of the strawhat pirates, he is a swordsman and his dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world by defeating the current greatest swordsman, Dracule Mihawk.

Sanji is the cook of the strawhat pirates, he is a martial artist and his dream is to find the all blue, a legendary sea that is said to be the home of all the fish in the world.
